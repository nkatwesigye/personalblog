The town blog captures the life of the town, the mysteries behind the blocks, and allies that live through the times. We show you the most pleasant and interesting corners, the spaces that come alive in the night and breed the spirit of the townThrough the texture of Style, freedom, courage, thrill and adventure, the town lives through us all and carries us on its bold shoulders.

Each dealt with their own hand, we move forward with a wash of hope and excitement to make our mark and leave a our print on the town. Still but yet alive as could be, the town serves us with its share of life that breeds yet more life for the next day.

Holding the sun every day on its forehead and the moon every night on its bare-back, we always have a light as we walk through the tunnel. Yes the town we come and go, we bless and curse but its spirit still remains faithful to our journeys.Work for our hands, food for our bellies, and life for our spirits, the town nourishes us through it all.

Let's rise to the call of life, the call of our destiny and breath life into the town, fearless and still tender we tread forward anchoring to the deep roots of the town.

The more of the town we open up to , the more beauty and goodness blossoms out of our lives
